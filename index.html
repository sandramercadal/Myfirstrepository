Emily in Paris


The Main Cast

EMILY ~ The Marketing Exec
played by Lily Collins

Emily's from Chicago, has a Masters in Communications but can't yet speak French. How will she adjust to the challenges of life in Paris while juggling her career, new friendships, and love life?
GABRIEL ~ The Chef
played by Lucas Bravo

A talented chef who works at L'Esprit de Gigi Restaurant located in the 5th Arrondissement of Paris. He also happens to be Emily's handsome yet complicated downstairs neighbour

CAMILLE ~ The Art Gallery Curator
played by Camille Razat

Parisian chic Camille grew up in Champagne, France and takes Emily under her wing with unexpected consequences SYLVIE ~ The Boss
played by Philippine Leroy-Beaulieu

Effortlessly chic Parisian boss Sylvie is definitely not always the boss you want to have ALFIE ~ The Love Interest
played by Lucien Laviscount

A banker from London who meets Emily at French classes. Devilishly handsome, he refuses to speak French or immerse himself in the French culture MINDY ~ The Singer
played by Ashley Park

Born in Shanghai Mindy is in Paris to escape joining her family's successful business back home. Will she make it on her own? PIERRE ~ The Fashion Designer
played by Jean-Christophe Bouvet

Haute couture fashion designer Pierre struggles to relate to the audience that is today's youth.
A cultural icon with disdain for social media. Can Emily turn his fortunes around? JULIEN ~ The Co-Worker
played by Samuel Arnold

Fashionable and outspoken Parisian Julien works at luxury marketing firm Savoir and is not too happy to get a new American co-worker dropped in his lap



Binge Worthy Pure Escapism That Will Feed Your Inner Wanderlust

Emmy nominated for Outstanding Comedy Series,
Emily in Paris is charming, romantic comedy entertainment in a setting that’s fun to look at.
It manages to raise the spirits and let's face,

who doesn't just love that!















Set in beautiful Paris, the show is written by Darren Star who also created Sex and The City.
The series stars Lily Collins as aspiring Marketing Exec Emily Cooper, who moves to Paris to provide
an American point of view to french Luxury Marketing firm 'Savoir'.
It's not just the scenery that is beautiful but the Fashion - a visual feast for those with an appreciation
for eclectic and stylish wardrobes. Think colourful, quirky and extravagant fashion choices!

Filming Locations
Whoever the filming location scout was for this show did a phenomenal job!
Say Bonjour to the dreamy Montmatre, Saint-Germain, Champs-Elysées and further afield to the beautiful regions of Provence, Versailles & the Loire Valley.

Emily in Paris' Apartment


Location: 5 Place de l’Estrapade
A must-see for all Emily in Paris fans is her apartment “Maison Moreau” where she lives on the fifth floor (without a lift). The grand windows of the building overlook a picturesque square surrounding a lovely fountain. Not far away is the florist shop where Emily and Camille first meet, the Panthéon and Gabriel’s Restaurant Terra Nera.

Locate here

Hotel Molitor

Location: 13 rue Nungesser et Coli
The colourful outdoor pool that Emily and Mindy visit in Season 3 is the iconic Molitor Hotel celebrating over 90 years in the city. Two Art Deco pools were opened in 1929 and have been restored to their former glory following a short hiatus. When you are next in town, make sure to check out the coolest summer hangout.

Locate here

Gabriel’s Restaurant – Bistro Terra Nera

Location: 17 Rue des Fossés Saint-Jacques
Bistro Terra Nera is acutally an Italian restaurant named Terra Nova near Place de l’Estrapade. They are open everyday for lunch and dinner except on Sundays and you have to reserve a table if you want to eat there, especially now that the show made it popular. For fans of the show, a meal and a dish named after Emily were added to the menu.

Locate here

Jardin du Luxembourg

Location: Rue de Médicis - Rue de Vaugirard
Emily's favourite place to go for a run is one of the most beautiful parks and gardens in Paris. Covering 25 hectares, it was created in 1612 at the request of Queen Marie de Medici. Featurung 106 statues, a forest, fountains, a large pond and a palace it really is not to be missed.

Locate here

Château de Sonnay - Tours

Location: Cravant-les-Côteaux
The fictional Château de Lalisse belonging to Camilles' family is actually Le Château de Sonnay in Chinon about 3 hours from Paris. The castle is a winery that’s been operating as such since the mid-1800's and is open to visitors. I can't wait to take a tour!

Locate here

Place Dalida - Rue de l'Abreuvoir

Location: Rue de l'Abreuvoir
In Season 1, Emily creates a marketing campaign for a brand of luxury mattresses in one of the best photo spots in Montmatre. Her idea is to put beds in public places so people can take selfies on them. This little quaint square is dedicated to French music icon Dalida and is on one of the most picturesque streets in Paris!
